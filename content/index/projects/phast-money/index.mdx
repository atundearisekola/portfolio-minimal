PhastMoney is an online platform that provides working capital finance to SMEs in Nigeria. PhastMoney offer flexible, short-term loans that can be used to purchase inventory, service new orders or optimize cash cycles.
PhastMoney aims at becoming the leading financing platform for SMEs, freelancers, and the self-employed. PhastMoney focus on customers that are hardly served by traditional providers and therefore requires a new approach to risk and credit scoring, Thanks to PhastMoney data-driven technology, PhastMoney provides fast, fair and uncomplicated financing to its customers.

#### __Tech Stack__
 React Native, Java(Springboot), Kafka.

#### __Architectural Pattern__
  PhastMoney web-backend was designed with a micro-service event-driven approach using Kafka as an event source.
And PhastMoney mobile application was developed with react-native, redux, and redux-thunk.
#### __Design Patterns__
  Our design patterns are based on two main principles of object-oriented design:
- Develop to an interface, not an implementation.
- Favor object composition over inheritance.
